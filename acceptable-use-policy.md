# Game Match Acceptable Use & Community Guidelines

_Last updated: December 14, 2025_

Game Match is designed to help players discover, organize, and enjoy sports together. To keep our community safe, respectful, and productive, all users must follow these Acceptable Use & Community Guidelines. These rules apply to all features, including messaging, reviews, waitlists, bookings, and support channels.

---

## 1. Core Principles
1. **Respect:** Treat fellow players, organizers, and staff with courtesy.
2. **Safety:** Prioritize physical and psychological safety for everyone.
3. **Integrity:** Be honest about your identity, skill level, and intentions.
4. **Fair Play:** Follow game rules, venue policies, and local laws.

---

## 2. Prohibited Content & Conduct
You may not engage in any of the following:

### 2.1 Abuse & Harassment
- Threats, bullying, stalking, or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory remarks about race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.
- Sexual harassment, unwanted advances, or explicit content.

### 2.2 Fraud & Misrepresentation
- Creating fake accounts or impersonating others.
- False claims about skill level, player counts, or session details.
- Attempting to manipulate waitlists, bookings, or reviews through deception.

### 2.3 Illegal or Dangerous Activities
- Organizing or encouraging violence, self-harm, or illegal activities.
- Sharing or distributing drugs, weapons, or contraband during sessions.
- Violating venue rules, health/safety regulations, or public event permits.

### 2.4 Spam & Malicious Behavior
- Sending unsolicited promotional content or mass messages.
- Uploading malware, phishing links, or intentionally disruptive content.
- Attempting to hack, reverse engineer, or interfere with the App’s operations.

### 2.5 Privacy Violations
- Sharing personal information about others without consent.
- Recording or photographing participants without permission where local law requires consent.

---

## 3. Reviews & Feedback
- Provide honest, constructive feedback based on firsthand experience.
- Do not post defamatory content, personal attacks, or retaliatory reviews.
- Report safety or policy concerns through in-app support rather than public reviews.

---

## 4. Organizer Responsibilities
Organizers must:
1. Clearly communicate session details, fees, and requirements.
2. Honor bookings or provide timely notice when cancelling.
3. Maintain safe environments and enforce venue rules.
4. Moderate participant behavior and report violations to Game Match.

---

## 5. Player Responsibilities
Players must:
1. Arrive on time, be respectful, and follow instructions.
2. Pay applicable fees and adhere to cancellation policies.
3. Use equipment safely and leave venues in good condition.
4. Report concerns about fellow players or organizers promptly.

---

## 6. Enforcement
- Game Match may issue warnings, remove content, or suspend/terminate accounts for violations.
- Severe or repeated misconduct can result in permanent bans and referral to law enforcement if required.
- We may limit platform features (e.g., messaging, bookings) while investigating reports.

---

## 7. Reporting & Support
If you witness or experience violations:

> Email: **support@gamematch.app**  
> In-app: Use the “Report” option where available or contact support via settings.

Provide relevant details such as session ID, time, screenshots, or messages. We review urgent safety issues within **24 hours**.

---

## 8. Appeals
If you believe an action against your account was in error, contact **appeals@gamematch.app** within 14 days. Include your account email and a clear explanation. Appeals are reviewed by a separate team to ensure fairness.

---

## 9. Updates to These Guidelines
We may update these guidelines to address new features or risks. Material changes will be communicated via the App or email. Continued use of Game Match indicates acceptance of the updated rules.

---

Thank you for helping us maintain a positive, inclusive community for all Game Match participants.
